About SSP

High schools in Jamaica need a system to efficiently assign student to classes based on their desired CAPE subjects as well as create timetables for sixth form students. Institutions wish to automate the subject selection and scheduling process for faster, more efficient results.

Subject Selection Pro is a web application made with efficiency in mind. With aims to solve the complex problems associated with automatically accepting eligible students based on CSEC subjects in addition to automatically generating a student's personalized timetable. With minimal staff input, Subject Selection Pro aims to minimize human error and computation time.
